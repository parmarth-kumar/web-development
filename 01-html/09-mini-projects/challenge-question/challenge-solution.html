Ada Lovelace

Pioneering Mathematician and Computer Programmer

About

Ada Lovelace is known for her work on Charles Babbage's mechanical general-purpose computer, the Analytical Engine. She was the first to recognize that the machine had applications beyond pure calculation.

Timeline of Accomplishments

  • 1815: Born in London, England.
  • 1833: Met Charles Babbage and began studying the Analytical Engine.
  • 1843: Published the first algorithm intended for processing on a machine.
