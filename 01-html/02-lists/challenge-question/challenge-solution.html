Classic Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 slices of cheese
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • Optional: tomato slices

Instructions

  1. Butter one side of each bread slice.
  2. Place one slice in a pan, butter side down, then add the cheese and optional tomato.
    • Keep the filling centered so it melts evenly.
    • Add a second cheese slice if you want a richer sandwich.
  3. Top with the second slice of bread, butter side up.
  4. Cook on medium heat until golden brown on both sides.
  5. Serve warm and enjoy.
